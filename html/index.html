What is MR?

  • Mixed reality refers to environments where real and virtual elements interact seamlessly and in real time, allowing users to interact with and manipulate both physical and digital components simultaneously.

How does MR interact with two different worlds (physical and virtual world)?

  • Track a person's body position in the physical world
  • objects, surfaces, and boundaries (spatial mapping and scene understanding)
  • ambient lighting and sound
  • object recognition
  • physical locations

MR vs AR vs VR

  • AR adds a layer of virtual objects to the real-world environment (no direct interaction between the virtual and physical world objects.)
  • VR fully-immersive virtual environment (nothing is from physical world)
  • MR vs AR—MR allows the interaction between virtual and physical objects in real time and AR can’t interact with virtual and physical objects.
  • MR vs VR—MR can have virtual and physical objects co-exists. VR blocks all the physical world objects and replaces them with virtual objects.
